🔎 First Things First

What is Right Form of Verb?

Right form of verb means picking the correct shape of the verb — based on tense, subject, voice, or condition — so the sentence sounds right. Simple, na?

Subject changes the verb

He goes to school.Singular subject (He) → verb + s/es
They go to school.Plural subject (They) → base verb

Tense and condition change the verb

I have completed my work.Present perfect → have/has + past participle
If I were a bird, I would fly.Imaginary condition → were + would + base verb
